Hello!

My name is Andrew Proctor and I’m an assistant professor at Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich.

I am especially interested in the use of structural and reduced form econometric methods to better understand human capital investment and the determinants of labor market inequality.

I was previously at PhD student at Stockholm School of Economics and a visiting PhD student at Duke University.

Before graduate school, I worked briefly in nonprofit and government on community development issues, particularly homelessness.
